# Disclaimer

**investment-discipline is a decision-discipline journal, not an investment
advisor.** Read this before using the tool.

## What this tool does not do

- It does **not** provide investment advice, recommendations, or buy/sell
  signals for any security, ETF, cryptocurrency, or other financial instrument.
- It does **not** predict prices, forecast markets, or value any specific asset.
- It does **not** manage money, execute trades, or connect to any brokerage.

These limits are not just promises in this document — they are hard-coded into
the skill instructions (`SKILL.md`) and the persona guidance (`personas.md`),
which forbid the assistant from expressing any opinion on a specific ticker or
price. The whole purpose of the tool is the opposite of advice: it only reflects
**your own previously written rules** back to you.

## Personas

The tool may respond in the voice of a well-known investor (e.g. Livermore,
Soros, Marks, Druckenmiller, Lynch, Duke). These personas are **educational
summaries of publicly stated investing philosophies**. They are **not** the
actual views, statements, or endorsement of those individuals, and no
affiliation is implied.
